About Me

My name is Joanne and I have a puppy named Potato. My favorite food, coincidentally (but not really), is also potatoes. Generally speaking though, I am a lover of all foods. I even went to school to become a chef but soon realized that I wasn't interested in the act of cooking so much as the act of eating.

I like to take long walks at grocery stores and binge eat at Indian lunch buffets when life stresses me out. I enjoy all forms of art especially literature and I secretly dream of living in Italy writing books and drinking wine (maybe beer from time to time, no, who am I kidding. Beer all the time) for the rest of my life.
